ABOUT US // WHAT WE BRING TO YOU





WHO WE ARE


CodeHub is a place to code, share, and learn from one another. If you are developing for the web and wish to exchange knowledge and skills, come and join us. We are largely based within NIT Rourkela, but anyone welcome. CodeHub is also a chapter of Google Developers Group.





MISSION


Codehub is trying to create a better coding infrastructure at NIT Rourkela which lacks talented coders as a result companies like top companies Google, Amazon, Facebook are not hiring any graduates from our institute.





VISION


We would train our members for ACM-ICPC Competition and help them built a strong fundamentals for competitive coding. We would help first year students who struggle with their grades and help them clear their concepts.





OBJECTIVE


To encourage the school students to take up more algorithmic based programming challenges and excel in this Olympiads. If we can increase their problem solving skills as well as give them much needed motivation for taking up their interests to next level. We would train this talented batch of selected students for the ACM-ICPC as well as IOI. This would not only help them enhancing their skills but would also give them exposure to the real competitive world. We are working on several projects involving future technology.
